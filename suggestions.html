Energy-Saving Suggestions

Reduce Lighting During Daytime

Switch off or reduce lighting in areas with ample natural light to save 50 kWh/day.

Optimize HVAC Systems

Ensure HVAC systems are turned off during non-working hours to save 100 kWh/day.

Use Energy-Efficient Appliances

Replace old appliances with energy-efficient models to save 200 kWh/month.
